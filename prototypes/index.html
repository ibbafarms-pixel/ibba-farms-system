🎨 Design Philosophy
Clean & Simple: The interface focuses on clarity and ease of use.
Responsive: Works on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices.
Consistent: Unified color scheme and component styling across all pages.
Accessible: Clear labels, good color contrast, and keyboard navigation support.
